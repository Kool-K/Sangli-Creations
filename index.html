Our Heritage

Welcome to Sangli Creations, where every artifact tells a story of India's glorious past.

Founded with a passion for preserving ancient craftsmanship, Sangli Creations brings the soul of Indian artistry to your modern home. From the intricate brass work of Moradabad to the vibrant textiles of Jaipur, our collection is a curated journey through the heart of India.

We believe that decor is not just about aesthetics; it is about emotion, culture, and the warmth of tradition. Whether it is a hand-carved Ganesha idol for your altar or a block-printed bedsheet for your sanctuary, we ensure authenticity in every thread and casting.
